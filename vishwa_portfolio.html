About Me

Architecture
with Purpose

I am an architecture student dedicated to innovative, sustainable, and climate-responsive design. My projects focus on earthy materials, passive strategies, and context-sensitive solutions to craft spaces that are meaningful, expressive, and engaging.

Through academic assignments, competitions, and real-world residential projects, I strive to blend conceptual ideas with practical design, ensuring a close relationship between people, nature, and the built environment.

"Design is not just what it looks like — it is how it feels to live within it."
